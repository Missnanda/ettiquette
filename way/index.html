The Miss Nanda Way

Welcome to a partnership crafted with care — where excellence meets respect, and growth is nurtured in every moment.

1. Time as a Gift

We start and end on time. A few minutes early sets the tone for focus and flow. Consistent attendance supports steady progress.

2. Space that Speaks

A clean, quiet corner—free of screens and noise—helps minds stay present and sharp. A cool, well-ventilated space is also highly recommended to maintain energy, comfort, and full focus throughout the session.

3. Tools in Hand, Mind Engaged

Books, iPads, and materials ready — on a desk that fits them all. A long, study desk (ideally 120 cm or more) allows everything to be within reach without crowding. When everything’s prepared, we can go further, faster.

4. Grace in Every Interaction

Politeness and kindness are expected from students and families alike. It’s how we keep learning gentle, joyful, and honest.

5. Speaking With Grace

Our language reflects our learning. We keep it clear, calm and kind.

Messages to Miss Nanda should be written with courtesy. A warm, respectful greeting and clear message go a long way in creating a thoughtful learning culture.

6. Warm Hellos & Goodbyes

Welcoming Miss Nanda with a smile and walking her to the door after sessions are small gestures that reflect big character. They help children practice confidence, gratitude, and well-rounded manners.

7. Curiosity, Always

We encourage questions, wonder, and the courage to say, “I don’t know yet.”

8. Room to Learn

Sessions are sacred space. Interruptions are gently minimized — focus deserves to be protected.

9. Dress the Part

Learning is a state of mind. Fresh clothes and upright posture tell the brain: I’m ready.

10. Trust the Process

Growth takes time. We celebrate little breakthroughs while building big outcomes.

11. Mutual Respect, Mutual Reward

We show up with commitment. Families who bring warmth, gratitude, and honesty help us do our best work.

12. Nourishment with Intention

To maintain clarity and energy during sessions, Miss Nanda follows a clean, sugar-free lifestyle. While we deeply appreciate the kindness of offering refreshments, we gently ask that only wholesome, low-sugar options be considered—if any. Thank you for supporting this rhythm of teaching.

13. After the Session

We encourage families to allow students a quiet moment to reflect or rest — letting the learning settle and sink in, rather than rushing into the next activity.

14. Privacy & Boundaries

Please respect Miss Nanda’s privacy outside of sessions. Personal life and teaching space are kept separate — honoring this boundary ensures trust and professionalism.

15. Reschedules & Session Changes

We allow one reschedule per month with at least 24 hours’ notice. Missed sessions without notice cannot be made up. Thank you for understanding when life brings the unexpected.

At Miss Nanda, etiquette is more than rules — it’s the quiet language of respect, care, and intention. When we greet kindly, speak thoughtfully, and show up with purpose, we create more than just lessons. We create a space where learning feels safe, graceful, and alive.

Here, every message matters. Every moment counts. Let’s build something beautiful — one mindful interaction at a time.
